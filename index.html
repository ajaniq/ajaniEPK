Born and raised in Seattle, AJANI's passion for music stems from a young age. Inspired by blank abd blank and blank he continues to find his sound everyday influenced by hip-hop, house, and global club sounds. His vision for every performance is to make any venue feel like a house party His vision is to match the energy of the audience whether the venue is big or small, and make everyone one big family.
